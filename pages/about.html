About

First of all, thanks for visiting my website! I am Ayan Shafqat, an Engineer, father of two amazing children, husband of a wonderful loving wife, and a seasonal musician. Tinkering has been a part of me for as long as I can remember. As a young child, I remember extracting electric motors from toy cars to make propellers for Styrofoam boats. Most of my time nowadays are spent working on embedded systems, and playing with my kids. I also sometimes pick up my guitar to jam with my wife. Life is Good.
