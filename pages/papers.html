Papers

A list of papers I have worked on that I am proud of.

  • The Challenges of Transcription of Music, NYU Poly (Fall 2011): Mentored by Professor Matthew Campisi, Creating and implementing algorithm for detecting pitch, and list its challenges.

  • Phase Vocoder: Pitch Shifting/Time-Stretching (STFT Based Algorithm), NYU Poly (Fall 2011): Mentored by Professor Ivan Selesnick: Time stretch and pitch shift an audio signal while preserving tonal characteristics of the audio signal.

  • DTMF Decoder Using Goertzel's Filter-bank, NYU Poly Mentored by Professor Gusteau Duclos, Encode and decode DTMF messages using Goertzel’s Filter-bank algorithm.
